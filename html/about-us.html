About Us

Our Story


The Fitness Club was founded in 2019 as a family owned and operated business. The Fitness Club founders didn’t want it to be just another gym - they wanted to be the best in the region and set their minds to doing so! Since its birth, The Fitness Club has grown into one of Wales' largest most reliable and trustworthy gyms and helped customers live longer, happier and healthier lives.


Our Core Values


    Achieving ultimate human performance

    Making sure everyone has fun

    Teamwork is dreamwork

    Quality of quantity

    Accountability and transparency

    Being compassionate to clients needs/wants



We’re committed to providing our nation with a safe and affordable place to get a great workout. Being physically and mentally fit has never been more important and we are proud of the role that we are able to play.


Whatever your starting point, and wherever you want to go, we can help.
